Adelaide Blockchain

For students by students - Education & Innovation

Who are we?

Adelaide Blockchain is a student run group based at the University of Adelaide.

We organise internal blockchain projects, host industry talks and run social events to engage anyone interested blockchain technology, especially students.

Adelaide Blockchain's biggest goal is to empower students, allumni, and blockchain enthusiasts throughout our community
and industry by instilling the technical and developmental know how needed to participate in the rapidly growing blockchain industry.

Our team consists of undergraduates from a variety of backgrounds: Electrical Engineering, Mechatronics Engineering, Physics,Computer Science, Economics, Business, and more.

Learn More

What do we do?

By hosting industry talks, workshops and developing projects throughout the Adelaide area, we hope to promote a community mindset focused upon innovation, communication, use cases and educations around blockchain technologies.
